What a Shopify Staff Pick means

Shopify uses its Staff Pick spotlight to highlight apps it wants merchants to notice in the App Store. That makes it a real third-party signal, not a label created by the developer.

When an app shows up there more than once, it suggests the product has kept feeling relevant and useful over time.
