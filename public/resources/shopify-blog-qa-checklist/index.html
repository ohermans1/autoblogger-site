Shopify Blog QA Checklist

Home/Resources/Shopify Blog QA Checklist

This checklist is designed for stores that want every Shopify post to ship with cleaner SEO structure and fewer avoidable misses.

Quick Takeaways

  • Title, meta, and heading review
  • Product and collection link checks
  • FAQ, imagery, and CTA review
  • Post-publish indexing and measurement steps

Pre-publish checks

The QA pass should focus on the structural details that often get missed when content is moving quickly. Those small misses add up to weaker snippets, weaker internal linking, and weaker conversion support.

A short consistent checklist is better than an exhaustive process no one follows.

  • Title, meta description, and heading hierarchy
  • Primary product or collection links
  • FAQ or proof blocks where the topic calls for them
  • CTA placement and image or media quality

Post-publish checks

Once the post is live, confirm the page renders correctly, links point to the right destinations, and the URL is ready for indexing and measurement. Post-publish QA is where template and publishing issues usually surface.

This step also makes later refresh work easier because the baseline is documented.

  • Confirm live rendering and internal links
  • Check indexing status and page discoverability
  • Record the page in the reporting or refresh tracker
  • Review early performance once impressions begin to appear

Action Checklist

  1. Run the pre-publish QA before every Shopify post goes live
  2. Confirm links, snippet, and CTA behavior on the live URL
  3. Log the post in the tracking sheet after publication
  4. Use performance data to improve the checklist over time
