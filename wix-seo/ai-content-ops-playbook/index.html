Wix AI Content Operations Playbook

Home/Solutions/Wix AI Content Operations Playbook

This playbook gives Wix site owners a weekly operating rhythm for SEO content execution.

At a glance

  • Plan weekly post themes around service intent
  • Standardize title and meta QA before publish
  • Link every article to one priority service page
  • Track CTR, rankings, and lead quality

Set a weekly operating rhythm

Wix sites benefit from the same discipline as any other content program: plan, publish, review, and refresh on a fixed rhythm. The main difference is which service pages and local or niche intents the content needs to support.

A lightweight weekly cadence is easier to keep than a large monthly sprint that slips every time priorities change.

  • Plan posts around service intent and recurring customer questions
  • Use a standard review step for title, meta, and headings
  • Link each article to one clear service or conversion page
  • Review impressions and lead quality by topic group

Keep content tied to leads, not just traffic

The purpose of the workflow is to create useful content that supports inquiries, bookings, or service page visits. Every article should make the next step obvious without turning the page into a sales pitch.

If a content topic cannot support a meaningful service path, it is usually lower priority.

  • Add service links where the reader expects the next step
  • Use FAQs to address objections before the contact stage
  • Refresh posts when services, offers, or proof points change
  • Measure both click quality and lead outcomes

A quick checklist

  1. Create a simple editorial calendar tied to core services
  2. Standardize snippet and internal-link QA before publishing
  3. Track which articles assist service-page visits or leads
  4. Refresh top-performing pages each quarter
